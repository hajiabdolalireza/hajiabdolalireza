## 🚀 What I Focus On

### 📌 Product Strategy & Product Management
I enjoy shaping products from problem definition to execution:
- Product vision and roadmap definition
- MVP thinking and success metrics
- KPI/OKR design and tracking
- Prioritization using frameworks like **MoSCoW**
- Agile execution and release planning
- Cross-functional coordination between product, operations, marketing, and tech

### 📊 BizOps, Operations & Profitability
A major part of my work is improving how businesses operate:
- Designing lightweight operational and financial systems
- Portfolio and profitability analysis
- Cost control and better resource allocation
- Turning messy operations into structured, trackable workflows
- Helping teams make better decisions with clearer business visibility

### 🧠 Data-Driven Decision Making
I like using data as a decision tool, not just a reporting layer:
- KPI definition and performance monitoring
- Management reporting
- Revenue/cost analysis
- Customer persona building
- User journey and touchpoint analysis
- Using business signals to guide product and operational decisions

### 🔄 Process Design & Automation
I’m interested in reducing manual work and increasing execution speed:
- Workflow design
- Process improvement
- Automation of repetitive tasks
- Using **AI tools** and **n8n** for operational leverage
- Building simple systems that improve team productivity and decision quality

### 🎯 Product Design & User Understanding
I care about product decisions being grounded in user understanding:
- UX research
- Persona definition
- User flow design
- Journey mapping
- Wireframe / prototype collaboration
- Improving UI/UX in alignment with business and product goals

### 🛠 Technical Prototyping & System Thinking
While my main direction is product and strategy, I also use technical skills to think better and prototype faster:
- Web app architecture
- System design
- Full-stack prototyping for testing ideas
- Working with **Go**, **JavaScript/TypeScript**, and **Python**
- Using tools like **Postman**, **Git/GitHub**, and **WordPress/Elementor**
